Listening

I had an interview for a summer internship earlier this week! The company was called Datascan and the culture was definitely one of the coolest that I had ever seen. The employees associates were all incredibly friendly and the team had great chemistry. The CFO came in and talked to all of us and I fell in love with one of the things he had to say. "Don't listen to respond, listen to understand". I probably liked th aphorism so much because it clearly points out a part of me that I'm trying to understand. There's a difference between carying on a conversation and having a meaningful discussion, and the difference comes down to how one listens. It's only through the latter that any meaningful work can get done.
